F.A.Q

What do I need to do if I want to participate?


You need an EOS account to create content and to influence. You can log in to the application using Scatter.

How will I get my reward?


The EOS tokens from future influencers will be sent directly to your EOS account. You don’t need to perform any withdraw action.

Can I post anything?


Inappropriate content might be unlisted from the website.

Do I need EOS tokens to participate?


It is free to participate as an author. However, if you’re an influencer, you need EOS tokens to send to reward authors and previous influencers.
